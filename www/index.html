Characteristics of Living Things

Organisms are living things
1. Celluar Organization
A cell is the basic unit of structure and function in a organism
Types of Cell Stucture Unicellular = An organism made up of only 1 cell.
Multicellular = An organism made up of MANY CELLS
2. Energy Use
All organisms use energy. Some ways they use energy are eating, moving, hunting, etc. The sun is the source of energy for all organisms. Plants use the process of photosynthisis to convert sunlight into energy. Watch the video to learn more!

3. Growth & Development
Growth:
Growth is when organisms get larger in size by creating new cells
Development:
This is when an organism changes during an organism's life to become a more complex organism.

4. Response to Stimulus
The things that organisms react to are called stimuli or stimulus. All organisms must mantain homeostasis to stay alive. To mantain homeostasis an organism must react to internal stimuli.

Asexual = Only one organism is needed to reproduce
Sexual = TWO ORGANISMS are needed to reproduce
